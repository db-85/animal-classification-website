Animal Image Classifier

Choose a file from your computer, and press the "Upload" button. The ML algorithm will try to identify the animal among 151 different species and display it in green.
To see all the potentially detectable species, please check the list of animal classes.


Disclaimer: This website has been designed for demo purpose only. The expected accuracy will depend of several factors, including the picture quality, size, etc. By uploading a picture, you provide explicit consent to process it for classification purposes.
